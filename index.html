Frequently Asked Questions

Where is DevConf 2026 taking place?

DevConf 2026 will be held at the Dhaka International Convention Center, running for three full days.

Do I need coding experience to attend?

No. Sessions range from beginner-friendly talks to advanced technical deep-dives, so all skill levels are welcome.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes, all talks are recorded and made available to attendees based on their ticket tier.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another attendee.

Is there a group discount for teams?

Yes, our Team plan covers up to 10 people at a discounted rate compared to individual Pro tickets.
